New feature: upload photos directly to tea profiles & brew sessions


Author: Daniel Radosa — Tags: update, feature, tea journal
Created: 8th February 2026 @ 17:42

Until now, photos in Teanote could only be added by linking external image URLs. Starting next week, you can upload photos directly to both tea profiles and individual brew sessions.

This makes it much easier to document leaf appearance, liquor color, teaware, or session aesthetic — all in one place, without relying on third-party image hosting. Uploaded images stay attached to your tea journal and help preserve each session as a complete visual memoir.
