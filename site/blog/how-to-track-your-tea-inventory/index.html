How to track your tea inventory (without spreadsheets)

Tea inventory tracking only works if it stays easy. The goal is not perfection — it’s remembering what you own, what’s aging, and what you want to re‑buy.

The minimum viable tea profile

  • Tea type + name
  • Vendor + purchase date
  • Storage notes (where/how you keep it)
  • One default brew setup (grams + temp)

Connect inventory to sessions

The secret is linking each brew session back to the tea profile. Then you can see: “this sheng was sharp in winter, softer in spring” — without guessing.
