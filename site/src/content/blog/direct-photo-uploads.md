Photos make a tea journal more than text: you remember leaf appearance, liquor color, teaware, and the whole mood of a session.
Teanote supports **direct photo uploads** to both tea profiles and individual brew sessions.

## What you can capture

- Dry leaf + wet leaf
- Liquor color across infusions
- Teaware setups
- Session vibes you want to remember
