These are **starting points**, not rules. Your leaf amount, vessel, and water matter.
The best approach is to log a session, then adjust one variable next time.

## Quick starting guide

- **Oolong**: 10s → 12s → 15s → 20s…
- **Sheng puer**: 8s → 10s → 12s → 15s…
- **Shou puer**: 10s → 12s → 15s → 20s…
- **Black**: 12s → 15s → 18s → 25s…
- **White**: 12s → 15s → 20s → 30s…
- **Green**: 6s → 8s → 10s → 15s…

## The one thing to always write down

Write what you changed and what happened.

Example: `Infusion 3 was thin → raised temp +2°C`.
