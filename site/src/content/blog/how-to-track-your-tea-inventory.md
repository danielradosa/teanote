Tea inventory tracking only works if it stays easy.
The goal is not perfection &mdash; it's remembering what you own, what's aging, and what you want to re‑buy.

## The minimum viable tea profile

- Tea type + name
- Vendor + purchase date
- Storage notes (where/how you keep it)
- One default brew setup (grams + temp)

## Connect inventory to sessions

The secret is linking each brew session back to the tea profile.
Then you can see patterns like: "this sheng was sharp in winter, softer in spring" &mdash; without guessing.
