    About Teanote

    Teanote is a web application for tea enthusiasts.

    Teanote features

    The main features are:

    1. brew timer (time your infusions / steeps)
    2. tea profiles (curate your personal inventory w/ all tea details)
    3. journaling (write notes about your fave teas / brews / brew presets)

    Features info

    • A tea can be linked to a brew preset
    • A brew preset and tea can be linked to a journal

    Frequently asked questions

    Why is there no lifetime pricing option?

    Because it wouldn't be sustainable. Teanote is meant to grow with you over time — you log & try new teas, change infusion times, experiment, make journal entries, and explore more as your tea journey evolves.

    A one-time payment model might seem like a good deal upfront, but it usually leads to apps becoming abandoned or rarely used (e.g. VSCO or Ulysses — they both had to switch to a subscription model eventually to keep up with server costs and survive). With a small ongoing subscription, Teanote can keep improving and you stay more engaged in using it regularly.

    Do you offer student or teacher discount?

    No.

    What are available payment methods?

    You can pay with any Visa, MasterCard or AmericanExpress card via Stripe.

    Is it secure to use my card information?

    With Teanote Stripe.com is used for online payments. Stripe has been audited and certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. That is the most strict level of certification available in the payment industry.
