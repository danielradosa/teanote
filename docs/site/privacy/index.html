    Privacy Policy

    Teanote (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is owned and operated by Daniel Radosa, with arrangements in Slovakia and Denmark. We are committed to respecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation, EU Regulation 2016/679).

    This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we collect it, how we use it, how you can exercise your rights, and your options to control your data. By accessing or using Teanote services at teanote.xyz, app.teanote.xyz, and our documentation at docs.teanote.xyz, you agree to the practices described here.

    We periodically review and update this policy to ensure GDPR compliance and better safeguard your data rights.

    1. Definitions

    • "Platform": The Teanote app, website, APIs, and related services.
    • "User": Anyone accessing or using Teanote.
    • "Services": All digital products, web, and hosting services provided by Teanote.

    2. Data We Collect

    2.1 Account & Authentication Data

    • Email address (info@teanote.xyz)
    • Profile data (optional pseudonyms or names)
    • Login credentials (hashed passwords)

    2.2 Usage Data

    • IP addresses
    • Browser type, device info
    • Interaction logs and analytics data
    • Cookies, site visits, and activity timestamps

    2.3 Payment Data

    • Payment card details processed securely by Stripe or LemonSqueezy
    • Billing address (only for tax purposes, stored securely via Stripe/LemonSqueezy)

    2.4 Support & Communications

    • Correspondence, support requests, and legal inquiries

    3. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

    Our data processing is grounded on GDPR principles:

    • Contractual Necessity: To provide, deliver, and maintain services.
    • Legal Obligation: To comply with tax and legal regulations.
    • Legitimate Interests: Improving service quality, security, and security auditing.
    • Your Consent: For optional data collection like marketing and analytics.

    You can withdraw consent anytime by contacting us at info@teanote.xyz.

    4. Your Rights Under GDPR

    In accordance with GDPR (art. 15–22), you have rights to:

    • Access your personal data.
    • Correct inaccuracies.
    • Request erasure (“right to be forgotten”).
    • Data portability.
    • Restrict or object to processing.
    • Withdraw consent.
    • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

    You can exercise these rights by contacting us at info@teanote.xyz. We will respond to your requests within 10 working days.

    5. Data Sharing and Security

    • Data is shared only with trusted third parties necessary for service delivery:
    • Railway (hosting services)
    • Stripe or LemonSqueezy (payment processors)
    • hostcreators.sk (website hosting)
    • We do not sell your data.
    • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

    6. Data Retention

    • We store your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined.
    • Data related to your account will be retained until you request deletion.
    • You may request data deletion at any time via info@teanote.xyz.

    7. International Data Transfers

    Your data may be stored and processed in Slovakia, Denmark, or other countries with GDPR adequacy decisions or similar protections in place.

    8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies, pixels, and similar tools to enhance your experience. You can manage your preferences through your browser settings. For detailed cookie management and related controls, see our Cookies Policy.

    9. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

    We do not use automated decision-making or profiling that impacts your rights under GDPR.

    10. Data Security and Breach Notification

    In the event of a data breach that impacts your personal data, we will notify you and relevant authorities within the GDPR-mandated timeline, 72 hours, where applicable.

    11. Contact & Further Information

    For privacy inquiries, data access requests, or complaints, please contact:

    Daniel Radosa
    Email: info@teanote.xyz
    Address: Norgesgade 40 1.sal, Esbjerg 6700, Denmark

    12. Regular Updates

    This Privacy Policy will be reviewed annually. We will notify you of material changes by updating the policy on docs.teanote.xyz/privacy.

    End of privacy policy
