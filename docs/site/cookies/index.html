    Cookie Policy

    Last updated: 4th November 2025 at 23:15 CEST

    Teanote (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our services, including teanote.xyz, app.teanote.xyz, and docs.teanote.xyz.

    This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, the types of cookies we use, how we use them, and your rights and choices regarding cookies.

    1. What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites or apps you visit. They help us provide, improve, and personalize your experience by remembering preferences, enabling basic functionality, and collecting analytics data.

    2. Types of Cookies We Use

    We use the following categories of cookies on Teanote services:

    a. Strictly Necessary Cookies

    These cookies are essential for the operation of our services. They enable core features like authentication, security, and session management. You cannot opt-out of these cookies as they are required for the service you request.

    b. Performance and Analytics Cookies

    These cookies collect anonymous information about how users interact with the website and app, such as page visits, user behavior, and load times. This data helps us improve performance and usability. Analytics tools may include providers such as Google Analytics with consent.

    c. Functional Cookies

    Functional cookies remember your preferences (e.g., language, theme) to personalize your experience.

    d. Marketing and Advertising Cookies

    We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

    3. Third-Party Cookies

    We only use third-party cookies from trusted partners who assist us with analytics and hosting:

    • Google Analytics (optional, with consent)
    • Stripe and LemonSqueezy for secure payment processing (no direct tracking here)
    • Hosting providers such as Railway and hostcreators.sk

    We disclose any third-party cookies and link to their privacy policies where applicable.

    4. Cookie Consent and Control

    Under GDPR and ePrivacy Directive, we require your explicit opt-in consent before placing any non-essential cookies.

    • On your first visit, a cookie banner explains cookie usage and seeks your consent.
    • You can accept, reject, or customize cookie preferences.
    • We block all non-essential cookies until you provide consent.
    • You can change or withdraw your consent any time via cookie settings accessible within our services.
    • Refusing non-essential cookies will not impact your access to core features.

    5. How to Manage Cookies

    You have full control over cookies through:

    • Your browser settings (to block or delete cookies)
    • Our cookie preference center (via the cookie banner or app settings)

    Please note that disabling necessary cookies may limit certain features or prevent the service from working properly.

    6. Cookie Duration

    Cookies may be session-based (deleted after you close your browser) or persistent (remain for a specified duration). Examples include:

    Cookie Type Duration
    Session cookies Until browser closed
    Analytics cookies Up to 2 years
    Functional cookies Up to 1 year

    7. Updates to This Cookie Policy

    We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version is always available at https://docs.teanote.xyz/privacy. Continued use of Teanote services signifies acceptance of updated policies.
