## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites or apps you visit. They help us provide, improve, and personalize your experience by remembering preferences, enabling basic functionality, and collecting analytics data.

***

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies on Teanote services:

### a. Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the operation of our services. They enable core features like authentication, security, and session management. You cannot opt-out of these cookies as they are required for the service you request.

### b. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information about how users interact with the website and app, such as page visits, user behavior, and load times. This data helps us improve performance and usability. Analytics tools may include providers such as Google Analytics with consent.

### c. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember your preferences (e.g., language, theme) to personalize your experience.

### d. Marketing and Advertising Cookies

We currently do not use marketing or advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy accordingly.

## 4. Cookie Consent and Control

Under GDPR and ePrivacy Directive, we require your explicit opt-in consent before placing any non-essential cookies.

- On your first visit, a cookie banner explains cookie usage and seeks your consent.
- You can accept, reject, or customize cookie preferences.
- We block all non-essential cookies until you provide consent.
- You can change or withdraw your consent any time via cookie settings accessible within our services.
- Refusing non-essential cookies will not impact your access to core features.
